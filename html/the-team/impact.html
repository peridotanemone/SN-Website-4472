Giving Back

We strive to give back to our community. Our members mentor robotics teams at multiple elementary and middle schools in the county and volunteer at FIRST LEGO League (FLL) events throughout the year. Two of our members have also started a student-led tutoring organization for both middle and high school students.

Members of SuperNOVA are leaders in the STEM community and their schools. Most of our members compete on multiple robotics teams, including VEX Robotics, ZERO Robotics, and SeaPerch Robotics. They are active in their schools in varsity athletics, honor societies, Code Quest, and CyberPatriot teams. We also have members attending Governor’s School @ Innovation Park as well as the summer residential Governor’s schools.
