About

Team 4472 is a FIRST robotics competition (FRC) team from Prince William County, Virginia. We allow high school students to learn industry-level engineering through fierce competition. Since our team was founded in 2013, we’ve secured major awards and advanced to the FIRST World Championship in Houston, Texas (2022).

Our Vision

SuperNOVA’s goal is to build a dedicated team of diverse members. We encourage each other to absorb new knowledge from other passionate members and mentors, creating an environment that celebrates science, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM).
